2.) Beast Guage

After reading the abilities, you may be wondering what beast gauge is. In this section, I will discuss abilities tied to beast gauge and introduce the gauge mechanic of ffxiv.

Beast gauge is utilized and increased with specific abilities (see table above). As you click your combo attacks, you will build up a gauge, which you use on various hard-hitting abilities. 50 is the amount needed for said abilities.

NEVER let your beast gauge sit at full, and use it at around 70. you want to save your beast gauge for damage buffs, but capping will result in a damage loss.

FAQ

What is the best tank?

Well, there is no "best tank", but a good starter tank is the warrior! Each tank has its own unique charm and should be considered.

Why do people call WAR a healer?

Warrior has an ability called bloodwhetting, and it is potent. The ability allows you to heal while doing damage, which, combined with your AOE abilities, gives you basically no damage taken for 8 seconds.

What is the most complex tank?

All the tanks are straightforward in how they are played. They just take a combination of reading the tool-tips and practice!
