Creative Innovation with Pure Imagination Labs

Through our strategic partnership with Pure Imagination Studios, the K-AIRES Center bridges the gap between storytelling, entertainment technology, and academic research. This collaboration enriches our lab ecosystem with new dimensions of media design, 3D animation, and immersive experience production.

Highlights of the Partnership

  • Joint development of immersive educational experiences and trainings
  • Co-creation of virtual environments for research in emotional engagement and narrative AI
  • Access to studio-grade production pipelines for student-led XR and AI research projects Together, we empower students and researchers to explore new frontiers in digital storytelling, game-based learning, and cinematic simulation design
