Labs and Facilities
State-of-the-Art Facilities-

Key Facilities Include:

- AR/VR Studio: A dedicated space for VR/AR prototyping, featuring motion tracking, haptic feedback systems, and spatial audio.

- AI Innovation Lab: Equipped with advanced GPU clusters and machine learning platforms for real-time AI model training and deployment.

- Human-Centered Design Studio: Supports UX testing and rapid iteration for immersive systems interfaces.

- COSM Mixed Reality Production Studio Dome: Enables content creation using photogrammetry, green screen, and 3D scanning technologies within the immersive 60 foot COSM 8K LED Dome
