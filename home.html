K-AIRES
Welcome to K-AIRES: The Kansas Advanced Immersive Research for Emerging Systems Center The K-State Salina K-AIRES Center is a pioneering academic research hub dedicated to advancing immersive technologies and artificial intelligence (AI). As a leading institution in extended reality (XR), machine learning and AI innovation, and cyber-physical systems, K-AIRES unites multidisciplinary faculty, students, and industry experts to shape the future of human-technology interaction. From virtual and augmented reality solutions to AI-powered decision systems, our work supports transformative applications in aerospace, manufacturing, education, entertainment, and more. Discover how we are redefining research through immersive learning, collaboration, and innovation. Explore our work. Be part of the future.

Certificates & Degrees

Spacial Computing

The Spatial Computing Design Undergraduate Certificate is a focused program that empowers students with the skills to develop applications and software for spatial computing environments, such as augmented reality and virtual reality.

Immersive Systems Design

Immersive Systems Design is a cutting-edge degree program that combines the technology of digital design, human-computer interaction, virtual and augmented reality, 3D modeling and animation, and intelligent computing systems architecture.

Extended Reality Systems Design

The Extended Reality Systems Design Undergraduate Certificate is a program that explores the design and development of immersive experiences using augmented, virtual and mixed reality technologies.

Integrated Systems Design and Dynamics

This graduate degree provides individuals an understanding of complex technological systems and the ability to think and work systematically when approaching industry problems. This skillset is becoming increasingly vital for employees hired in a variety of technical, engineering, science, and business positions.
